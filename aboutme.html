Academic Life

Hi! I'm a Public Policy and Data Science student with a strong interest in how data, institutions, and political systems interact. I’m especially drawn to questions about governance and political polarization, and I enjoy using quantitative tools to better understand complex policy problems.

Currently, I’m building my academic and professional portfolio through coursework in economics, government, philosophy, Spanish, and applied data analysis. Through research projects and policy writing, I’ve developed experience analyzing legislation, evaluating policy tradeoffs, and communicating complex ideas clearly. I’m particularly interested in how evidence-based research can inform public decision-making, and I value precision, clarity, and strong argumentation in my work.
